                                            <h1>Eligibility Criteria &amp; Documentation for Business Loan</h1>

  <h3>Who can apply for Business Loan</h3>

 <p>Self Employed including Individual Proprietors, Private Ltd. Co. and Partnership Firms involved in the Business of Manufacturing, Trading or Services.</p>                                         

<h3>Business Loan Requirements:</h3>

 <ul class="listnone bullet bullet-check-circle-default">

<li>Minimum Turnover of Rs. 40 Lakhs.</li>

<li>Years in Business: Minimum of 3 years in current business and 5 years total business experience</li>

<li>Business must be profit making for the last 2 years</li>

<li>Minimum Annual Income (ITR): Rs. 1.5 Lakhs p.a.</li>

<li>Age of Applicant: Min 21 years &amp; Max. 65 years at the time of loan maturity.</li>

<h3>Documents Required for Business Loan :</h3>

<ul class="listnone bullet bullet-check-circle-default">

<li>PAN Card – For Company /Firm/ individual.</li>

<li>Proof of your identity: Copy of Aadhaar Card/Passport/Voter's ID card/Pan Card/Driving License.</li>

<li>Proof of your address: Copy of Aadhaar Card/Passport/ Voter ID-Card/Driving License.

Bank Statement (latest 6 months)</li>

<li>Latest ITR along with computation of income, Balance sheet and Profit &amp; Loss a/c for the last 2 years. Financial should be CA Certified /Audited.</li>

<li>Proof of continuation (ITR/Trade license /Establishment /Sales Tax certificate)</li>
